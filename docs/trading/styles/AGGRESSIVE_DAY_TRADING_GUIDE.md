## Trade Management

### Entry Checklist

Before entering an aggressive day trade:

- [ ] **Confluence score** validates signal (>35 SHORT, <65 LONG)
- [ ] **Volatility** suitable (ATR indicating 2-5% potential move)
- [ ] **Time availability** to check in 1-2 hours
- [ ] **Liquidity** sufficient (tight spreads, good volume)
- [ ] **Market session** active (avoid dead zones)
- [ ] **Stop loss** placed immediately after entry
- [ ] **Targets** pre-set in trading platform

### Position Management

#### Stage 1: Entry (0-30 minutes)
```
‚úÖ Enter at confluence signal
‚úÖ Set stop loss immediately: +2.22% (SHORT) or -1.82% (LONG)
‚úÖ Set Target 1 alert: -2.66% (SHORT) or +2.40% (LONG)
‚úÖ Note entry time and price
‚úÖ Set 1-hour check reminder
```

#### Stage 2: First Hour (30-90 minutes)
```
üîç Check position status:
   - If approaching T1: Prepare to close 55%
   - If against you: Confirm stop still valid
   - If ranging: Let it develop

üìä Adjust if needed:
   - Tighten stop to breakeven if >50% to T1
   - Cancel if market structure breaks
```

#### Stage 3: Target 1 Hit (1-2 hours)
```
üí∞ Close 55% of position
‚úÖ Move stop to breakeven on remaining 45%
‚úÖ Set Target 2 alert
‚úÖ Let runner work with zero risk
```

#### Stage 4: Target 2 Hit (2-4 hours)
```
üí∞ Close 30% more (now 85% closed total)
‚úÖ Move stop to T1 on final 15%
‚úÖ Set Target 3 alert
‚úÖ Runner now in pure profit mode
```

#### Stage 5: Target 3 or EOD (4+ hours)
```
üí∞ Close final 15% at T3 or end of day
‚úÖ Log trade in journal
‚úÖ Calculate actual R:R achieved
‚úÖ Note what worked/didn't work
```

### Exit Rules

**Mandatory Exits:**
1. ‚ùå Stop loss hit ‚Üí Exit 100% immediately
2. ‚è∞ End of trading day ‚Üí Close if not at T2+
3. üì∞ Major news event ‚Üí Close or hedge
4. üìâ Market structure break ‚Üí Exit early

**Profit Taking Rules:**
1. ‚úÖ T1 hit ‚Üí Take 55%, move stop to BE
2. ‚úÖ T2 hit ‚Üí Take 30%, move stop to T1
3. ‚úÖ T3 hit ‚Üí Take final 15%
4. ‚è∞ 75% to T1 in <1h ‚Üí Consider early partial close

**Never:**
- ‚ùå Move stop loss further away (accept loss or close)
- ‚ùå Add to losing position (no averaging down)
- ‚ùå Hold overnight without considering risk
- ‚ùå Skip profit taking at T1 (lock in gains!)

## When to Use

### Perfect Market Conditions

‚úÖ **Use Aggressive Day Trading When:**

1. **Market Volatility: Moderate to High**
   - ATR indicating 2-5% daily moves
   - Not choppy/ranging
   - Clear directional bias forming

2. **Time Availability:**
   - Can check charts every 1-2 hours
   - Active during trading session
   - Not on vacation/meetings all day

3. **Market Session:**
   - NY/London overlap (high liquidity)
   - Asian session if crypto-focused
   - Avoid dead zones (Sunday evening, holidays)

4. **Technical Setup:**
   - Clear support/resistance levels
   - Confluence of multiple indicators
   - Clean chart structure

5. **Personal State:**
   - Well-rested and focused
   - No major distractions
   - Trading mindset ready

### Avoid Aggressive Day Trading When:

‚ùå **Don't Use When:**

1. **Market Conditions:**
   - Extremely choppy/ranging market
   - Major news event pending (FOMC, CPI)
   - Holiday period (low volume)
   - Flash crash conditions

2. **Personal Factors:**
   - Can't monitor for 1-2 hour intervals
   - Emotionally compromised
   - On losing streak (>3 losses)
   - Tired or distracted

3. **Account Status:**
   - Down >3% for the day
   - Recent large loss (taking break)
   - Below risk threshold

**A:** Switch to day trading (4-8h holding) or swing trading (daily checks). Don't force aggressive day trading if you can't monitor properly.
