How to Read This Chart
The heatmap shows predicted liquidation levels based on estimated positions at various leverage levels. Green zones below current price indicate long positions that would be liquidated if price drops. Red zones above current price indicate short positions liquidated if price rises. Brighter colors = higher liquidation volume.
Trading Applications
Use liquidation clusters as potential support/resistance levels. Large liquidation zones often act as magnets for price action. Cascade risk increases when price approaches dense liquidation clusters, as triggered liquidations can cause rapid price movements.
Demo Data Notice
This visualization uses live market data from exchanges. Liquidation levels are calculated based on current price and typical leverage distributions (5x, 10x, 20x, 50x, 100x). Data refreshes when you change symbol or timeframe.
